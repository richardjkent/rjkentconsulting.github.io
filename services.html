About Richard Kent

Director and Principal Consultant
RJ Kent Consulting

Richard Kent is the Director and Principal Consultant of RJ Kent Consulting, with over 15 years of experience working with industry, non-profit organisations and multilateral institutions at the intersection of natural resources, energy transition, sustainable finance and responsible mineral supply chains.

Richard specialises in helping organisations identify, understand and manage human rights, social and environmental risks associated with complex projects, operations and global value chains.

Professional Experience

His work includes human rights due diligence, ESG assessments, stakeholder engagement and social risk analysis for mining operations, processing facilities and integrated supply chains across Africa and Latin America.

Richard has undertaken assessments involving human rights impacts, community relations, involuntary resettlement, corruption risks and social grievances in challenging operating environments.

Areas of Expertise

  • Human Rights Due Diligence
  • Responsible Mining and Mineral Supply Chains
  • Energy Transition and Critical Minerals
  • ESG and Social Risk Assessment
  • Stakeholder Engagement and Community Relations
  • Sustainable Finance and Responsible Investment

Independent Advisory Approach

RJ Kent Consulting provides independent specialist expertise to support organisations seeking practical solutions to complex sustainability and responsible business challenges.
