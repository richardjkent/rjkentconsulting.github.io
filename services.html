Services

Independent advisory support on human rights, social impacts, natural resource development and responsible supply chains.

RJ Kent Consulting provides specialist advisory services to companies, investors, civil society organisations and multilateral institutions navigating complex human rights and sustainability challenges.

The work combines field research, stakeholder engagement, policy analysis and strategic advisory support to understand and address social risks associated with extractive industries and global mineral supply chains.

Human Rights
Investigations

Field-based investigations into human rights impacts in extractive industry contexts, including documentation of community-level impacts, interviews with affected populations, and analysis against international human rights standards.

  • Field Research
  • Human Rights
  • Extractive Industries

Community Mediation
& Grievance Processes

Facilitation and mediation with local communities experiencing social grievances related to industrial operations, including aspirational development processes and resettlement support.
