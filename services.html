Services

Independent advisory support on human rights, social performance, responsible mining and sustainable resource development.

RJ Kent Consulting supports companies, investors and organisations operating in complex environments to identify social risks, strengthen responsible business practices and develop practical approaches to sustainability challenges.

RJ Kent Consulting supports companies, investors, non-profit organisations and international institutions to identify and address social, environmental and human rights risks associated with complex projects and supply chains.

Private Sector Advisory

Supporting mining companies, energy organisations, technology companies and investors navigating increasing expectations around responsible business, sustainability and social performance.

Human Rights Due Diligence

Independent assessments of human rights risks across operations, projects, processing facilities and global value chains. This includes desktop research, stakeholder engagement, site assessments and practical recommendations for risk mitigation.

ESG and Social Risk Assessments

Assessment of social and environmental risks associated with projects, operations and investments, including community impacts, governance risks and responsible business practices.

Responsible Mining and Critical Minerals

Advisory support for organisations working across mining and mineral supply chains, including challenges linked to responsible sourcing, energy transition and the growing demand for critical minerals.

Stakeholder Engagement and Community Relations

Supporting organisations to build constructive relationships with affected communities and stakeholders through engagement strategies, dialogue and conflict-sensitive approaches.

NGO and Multilateral Organisation Support

Providing specialist research, advisory and implementation support to organisations working on responsible mining, human rights, sustainability and development initiatives.

Research and Technical Advisory

Supporting programmes through research, analysis, policy development and technical expertise on responsible business and sustainability challenges.

Training and Knowledge Development

Designing workshops and knowledge resources on human rights due diligence, responsible sourcing and social risk management.
